Shakespeare Random Story Generator


In effect, the random story that will be generated uses a shortened version of a Shakespeare eque story to pick out five speakers and determine what words they'd each say. We use the markov chain process to predict which speaker follows the other based on the story's sequence to generate the sequence of the speakers and we use the same algorithm to determine the words they'd each say from the speeches of each speaker in the story. The resulst are displayed whenever you click on Generate story.
